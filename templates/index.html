About Zavana

Zavana is your one stop stock analysis website that leverages the power of machine learning to make smart predictions and uses the following components to do this:

  • Daily news sentiment analysis of the stock.
  • Prediction using Neural networks.
  • Technical and fundamental analysis of the stock.

We combine all these aspects and give you the best possible prediction and information about the stock, to know more about how this website works, you can click on the button below.

Product Description

If you want to invest smartly then this is the right place for you. We provide a wide range of services for you to make financial progress.

  • 01 Stock Prediction

    The website predicts the stock price using Machine Learning models on the past data and the sentiment associated with the stock from news headlines with a good accuracy.

  • 02 Stock Analysis

    Graphs and tables on the stock Data and the company fundamentals are displayed for you to analyse and invest in the right stocks.

  • 03 Stock Recommendation

    The product also recommends you to whether buy or sell a stock based on different technical indicators.
